Step 3: Basic Stitches

Magic Ring / Magic Circle (mr)

Wrap yarn around your fingers to form a loop. Insert hook, pull up a loop, chain 1, crochet into the ring, then pull the tail to tighten.

Chain Stitch (ch)

The foundation of most crochet projects. Make a slip knot and pull yarn through loop repeatedly.

Single Crochet (sc)

Insert hook, yarn over, pull through, yarn over again, pull through both loops.

Half Double Crochet (hdc)

Yarn over, insert hook, yarn over and pull through. You now have three loops. Yarn over again and pull through all three loops.

Double Crochet (dc)

Yarn over, insert hook, pull through, yarn over pull through 2 loops twice.
